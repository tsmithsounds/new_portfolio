CREATIVE MARKETING

YELLOW WOOD

My role as marketing manager at Yellow Wood involved print & digital ad design, social media management, and event coordination. As a premier outdoor gear & apparel retailer whose focus is on ethical & sustainable manufacturing and social responsibility, my goal was to reflect these qualities in their marketing efforts.

The "Shop with Purpose" campaign ran in Fall 2016 to promote the benefits of supporting the variety of socially responsible brands carried at Yellow Wood. Examples shown are a quarter page newspaper layout and direct mail flyer.

Yellow Wood is focused on cause marketing and I'm grateful to have gained experience in the outdoor industry through working with them. I designed promotional content on digital & traditional media platforms, organized events with local businesses, non-profits, and vendors, and oversaw system operations and sales analysis. My marketing efforts with the Yellow Wood team contributed to a "Best New Business" award in Whitefish Bay, "Best of Milwaukee" finalist, and being the highest rated outdoor gear store in Milwaukee across online reviews.


I want to continue working with small businesses who are driven by a positive purpose. I hope to get to know your purpose and work together spread your message to an eager audience through thoughtful graphic design and marketing.
